  • Gender: Male or Female.

Summary of Insights

Price ($): A general increase over the months, from $12.8k in Jan 2023 to $54.3k in Dec 2023. However, there are 2 main anomalies; the lowest sales happened in Feb 2023 at $11.8k, and there was a steep drop in sales between Sep and Oct. It was a significant 45.5% decrease in sales during that period. Fortunately, numbers increased greatly once more to $51.6k in sales in Nov 2023.

After 2022, gross annual sales increased by 23.6%.

Gender: 78.6% of customers who purchased a car was male.

Dealer_Region: The best performing regions with car dealerships were Austin and Janeville. Respectively, they had U-Haul CO and Progressive Shippers Cooperative Association No as their top dealerships.

Company: Chevrolet was the top car brand being the most popular among buyers in 2023, topping at over 1,000 of its units sold. Dodge comes in at close second, selling 94 units less.

Recommendations & Next Steps

  • Investigate why sales dropped in Oct 2023. Then, expand on that analysis by exploring the possibility that it may be due to a disruption in the automobile market. Determine whether the problem identified is within the company’s control.
  • Consider creating a more inclusive customer experience for women at selected car dealerships in Austin and Janeville. By capturing a wider audience, it could potentially boost the company’s market share.
  • Reallocate resources to promote less popular car brands in the top 5 list, like Volkswagen and Mercedes-Benz. Ensure wise usage of resources by assigning them to products that need them more than their bestselling counterparts.
